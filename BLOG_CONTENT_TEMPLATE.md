## 🎯 SEO BEST PRACTICES FOR BLOGS

### Title Optimization
- **Format:** [Primary Keyword] | [Value Proposition] – TARK AI
- **Length:** 50-60 characters
- **Example:** "AI Career Guidance for Students | Smart Planning – TARK AI"

### Meta Description
- **Length:** 150-160 characters
- **Include:** Primary keyword, call-to-action, value proposition
- **Example:** "Discover how AI career guidance helps students make smarter career decisions. Get personalized roadmaps and expert insights. Start your journey today!"

### Heading Structure
```
H1: [Title with Primary Keyword]
  H2: [Section Title with Secondary Keyword]
    H3: [Subsection]
  H2: [Another Section]
    H3: [Subsection]
  H2: FAQ
    H3: [Question 1]
    H3: [Question 2]
```

### Keyword Density
- **Primary Keyword:** 1-2% density (15-30 times in 1500 words)
- **Secondary Keywords:** 0.5-1% each
- **Natural placement:** First paragraph, headings, conclusion
- **Avoid:** Keyword stuffing

### Internal Linking
**Minimum 3-5 internal links per blog:**
1. Link to AI Career Guider: "AI career guidance platform"
2. Link to Programs: "AI education programs"
3. Link to About: "AI EdTech company in India"
4. Link to Contact: "get in touch"
5. Link to other blogs (when available)

### External Linking
- **2-3 authoritative external links**
- Link to industry reports
- Link to research studies
- Link to news articles
- **Always:** `target="_blank"` and `rel="nofollow"`

### Image Optimization
**For each image:**
- **File name:** `ai-career-guidance-students-studying.jpg` (descriptive, keyword-rich)
- **Alt text:** "Students using AI career guidance platform to plan their future"
- **Size:** Compressed to < 200KB
- **Format:** WebP preferred, JPEG acceptable
- **Dimensions:** Max 1200px width

## ✍️ WRITING GUIDELINES

### Opening Paragraph
- **First 100 words:** Include primary keyword 2-3 times
- **Hook:** Start with a question, statistic, or compelling statement
- **Promise:** Tell readers what they'll learn
- **Example:**
  > "Are you struggling to choose the right career path? You're not alone. Over 70% of students report feeling uncertain about their career decisions. That's where AI career guidance comes in. In this comprehensive guide, we'll explore how AI-powered career counseling is transforming the way students plan their future, and how you can leverage these tools to make smarter career decisions."

### Body Content
- **Paragraph length:** 2-4 sentences max
- **Sentence length:** 15-20 words average
- **Transition words:** Use abundantly (however, moreover, therefore, etc.)
- **Voice:** Active voice preferred
- **Tone:** Professional but conversational
- **Data:** Include statistics and research when possible

### Conclusion
- **Recap:** Summarize key points
- **CTA:** Clear call-to-action with internal link
- **Next step:** Guide reader to specific action
- **Example:**
  > "AI career guidance is no longer the future—it's the present. By leveraging intelligent assessment tools and personalized roadmaps, you can make informed career decisions that align with your goals and market opportunities. Ready to get started? [Explore our AI career guidance platform](/ai-career-guider) and take the first step toward your dream career today."
